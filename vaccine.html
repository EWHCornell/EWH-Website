Passive Vaccine Refrigerator

It is estimated that more than a million children die annually from diseases that can easily be prevented by vaccination. Although millions of vaccines are donated to developing countries each year, about half of these are never administered due to temperature spoilage. Rural areas experience the worst difficulties with long-term vaccine refrigeration due to sparse and unreliable electricity. We are working towards developing a prototype for a passive refrigeration unit that will last around ten days. This system will primarily rely on layers of insulation to prevent heat loss and a specialized design to minimize exposure to surrounding air when the fridge is opened.
